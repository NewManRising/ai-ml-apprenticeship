1) **What are coefficients?**
Coefficients are weights applied by the model that signal importance.
2) **What does a positive coefficient mean?**
It means this particular feature has a strong(er) predictive quality than negative coefficients. 
3) **What does a negative coefficient mean?**
A negative coefficient means that this particular feature has a negative or lower predictive quality than other features.
4) **Which feature influenced your model most?**
Age influenced the model most. I was predicting "high earners" and age correlates to experience/tenure.
